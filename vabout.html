About Us

Who we are

We Love Our Customers

At Oncall Tours and Travels Pvt. Ltd., we are passionate about providing exceptional transportation services that cater to the unique needs of our valued clients. Under the leadership of our Managing Director, Mr. Jhamak Bhandari—who has over 18 years of expertise in the vehicle rental sector—we have built a reputation for reliability, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to delivering top-notch services has earned the trust of prominent organizations such as ADRA Nepal, Oxfam Nepal, A2J Nepal, Ipass Nepal, and many more.
With a diverse fleet of over 50 vehicles, we proudly serve a wide range of sectors including NGOs, INGOs, multinational corporations, and industries such as hydroelectricity and construction. Our team of dedicated professionals is driven by a shared goal of ensuring every journey is smooth, efficient, and tailored to our clients’ needs.
As we continue to grow, we remain focused on expanding our services within the tourism industry, both domestically and internationally, offering even more high-quality transportation solutions. At Oncall, our customers come first, and we are committed to going above and beyond to provide an unmatched travel experience, no matter the destination.
